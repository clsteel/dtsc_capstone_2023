Work Experience

Research Associate, Children's Mercy Hospital Kansas City - Center for Children's Healthy Lifestyles and Nutrition

December 2017 - present
I am the project coordinator for a research team led by Dr. Jordan Carlson at the Center for Children's Healthy Lifestyles and Nutrition that studies physical activity in youth. I am responsible for developing research study materials including participant materials, IRB protocols, staff procedure manuals, and data collection tools as well as building and maintaining the study team website. Additionally, I write manuscripts for journal publication as well as manage, clean, analyze, and synthesize study data for publication and distribution. I have experience analyzing GPS, ActiGraph, and activPAL data in various ways including trip detection and GIS analysis. My work also includes analyzing data from consumer physical activity devices (including Garmin and Fitbit smart watches). I have extensive experience building survey instruments digitally, specifically using REDCap, and frequently am responsible for analyzing and reporting survey results both for scientific publication and for public dissemination. I am responsible for communicating with research collaborators and ensuring that Good Clinical Practice is carried out while recruiting and enrolling participants in studies, including supervising and assisting students who work alongside me. I use R, python, and SPSS regularly and have previous experience with SAS and Mplus.

Pharmacy Technician, University of Kansas Hospital

November 2013 - May 2018
I compounded intravenous drugs including perioperative anesthesia and chemotherapy. Additionally, I helped develop procedures for compliance with regulatory bodies and patient safety initiatives to prevent medication errors. I was responsible for ordering medications to restock satellite pharmacies, filling dispensing systems, tracking expiration dates, delivering medications to nurses, physicians, and surgeons, supplying rescue medications to emergency situations, and handling and tracking controlled substances according to federal law and hospital policy. I held a Pharmacy Technician Certification Board credential from 2013-2018 and was registered with the Kansas State Board of Pharmacy.
